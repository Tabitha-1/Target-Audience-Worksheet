Discover Your Ideal Audience

Let's explore who you're creating your inspiring "faceless quotes" for. Thinking about these questions will help you connect with the wonderful people you want to reach!

Who is that one person I really want to reach with my message?
How old are they, generally?
What might their day look like?
What kinds of things are they really interested in, besides inspiring quotes?
What are some things they might be struggling with or finding challenging right now?
What kind of hopes and dreams do they have for themselves?
What kind of things do they already look at or follow on social media?
What values are important to them?
How can my "faceless quotes" pop up on their screen and make their day just a little bit better?

Your Audience Snapshot

Once you've filled out the questions above, your answers will appear here. You can then copy this text and paste it into a chatbot (like the ones we discussed!) and ask it to help you write a description or prompt about your target audience for your content planning.

Take your time with these questions. The clearer you are on who you're talking to, the more your message will resonate!
